How it all started
1. 2017
– The Frustration Begins
I was stuck in a low-paying $35K IT tech job. Overworked. Underpaid. No clear path forward.
2. 2018
– Trying to Find a Way Out
I bounced between certifications and online courses, but none of them got me interviews. I started questioning if tech was even worth it.
3. 2019
- The Turning Point
I stopped chasing certs and started building hands-on cloud projects real experience companies actually wanted.
4. 2020
– The Breakthrough
I finally cracked the code and landed my first real cloud engineering role. My income jumped from 35K to $135K overnight.
5. 2021
– Job Stacking Begins
With the right system, I added contract after contract under my cloud engineer consulting company hitting $489K/year across multiple remote opportunities.
6. 2022 –
Building the Program
Friends kept asking how I did it. I started mentoring others and they started winning too.
7. 2023
– Launching CloudBootcamp.AI
I launched a mentorship-based 8-week program designed to help others skip the guesswork and break into cloud
8. 2024
– 340+ Students Transformed
Our students have gone from stuck in Help Desk or non-tech jobs to making $130K–$200K+ remotely as Cloud Engineers.
